About

I am a creative writer seeking a career that allows me to employ my reading, writing, and research skills, as well as my passion for marketing! As a marketing intern for an independent publisher and a writer for a local arts magazine, I have honed the skills I have learned as an English major with a concentration in creative writing.

In addition to my English studies I have taken courses in statistics, social and digital media, and entrepreneurship. These courses have given me an understanding of statistical mathematics, which are useful in practically every field; social media marketing, primarily the use of Instagram and Facebook advertisements; and business basics, including a base knowledge of how to begin, operate, and manage a startup company.

I have experience copywriting, working in both the legal and financial fields, writing creatively for my classes, and writing professionally for both statistic and entrepreneurship classes. I have a passion for traveling and maintain a travel blog, along with a travel journal that includes photos I take. My fictional works have been published, and I maintain a blog with updated writing samples.
